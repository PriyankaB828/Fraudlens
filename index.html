🔍 FraudLens – Scam Detector

✨ What is FraudLens?

FraudLens is a smart scam detection tool that helps people stay safe on social media. It scans messages, links, and posts to identify suspicious or fraudulent content in real time.

✨ Why is it Needed?

Millions of people are targeted by scams daily through WhatsApp, Instagram, and emails. FraudLens helps detect suspicious keywords, risky links, and brand misuse to protect users before it’s too late.

✨ How Does It Work?

It uses a list of scam keywords (like “click here”, “you won”, “verify”, etc.), detects risky domains (like .tk, .ru), and alerts if known brand names (like “SBI”, “Aadhaar”) appear in suspicious context.

✨ Key Features

  • Scan messages/links
  • Detection using risky domains and scam keywords
  • Brand alert (e.g. "SBI", "Aadhaar")
  • Safety tips based on result
  • History saved in localStorage
